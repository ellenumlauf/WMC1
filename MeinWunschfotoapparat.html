My Dream Camera

Even though a good photographer can take great photos with any camera, a good camera makes things a lot easier and can even make some shots possible in the first place.


Personally, I don’t care much about the brand, as long as the camera meets a few basic requirements, is easy to use without endless menu clicking, and feels good in my hands. You’d be surprised how important that last part is—if a camera feels right, you’re much more likely to enjoy using it.

Requirements

  1. Resolution of the sensor: > 20 MP
  2. superfast focus
  3. Dust Removal System/integrated Camera Cleaning System (="WackelDackel")
  4. flip and rotate monitor
  5. at least 10 frames per second in burst mode
  6. ISO-Range: ISO 100 to at least ISO 102400
  7. Lenses:
    1. covers the telephoto range: 60 mm - 400 mm
    2. covers the wide angle range: 10 mm - 40 mm
